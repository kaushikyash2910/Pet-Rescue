🐾 Why Adopt from PawsHeart?

Every animal deserves a second chance at life. When you adopt from PawsHeart, you’re not just getting a pet — you're giving love, safety, and hope to a soul that truly needs it. Our adoption process is designed to make sure each rescue finds the perfect forever home. Plus, 100% of our proceeds go to helping more animals in need.

📄

1. Submit Application

Fill out our easy online application so we can match you with your perfect pet.

🕵️‍♀️

2. Home Visit & Screening

We ensure every pet goes to a safe and loving home through a quick screening process.

🤝

3. Meet & Greet

You get to meet the animal in person (or virtually) before making the final decision.

🏡

4. Welcome Home

Once approved, you’ll take your new furry friend home to begin a new chapter!
